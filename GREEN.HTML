PAGE 1: INTRODUCTION TO GREEN BOOSTER

Definition

Green Booster is an agricultural growth enhancer used to improve plant growth, increase crop productivity, and promote healthy plant development.

Importance of Green Booster

  1. Enhances plant growth.
  2. Improves crop yield.
  3. Strengthens plant roots.
  4. Promotes healthy leaves and stems.
  5. Improves resistance to diseases.
  6. Enhances nutrient uptake.
  7. Improves crop quality.
  8. Supports faster maturity.
  9. Reduces nutrient deficiencies.
  10. Increases farm productivity.

Objectives of Using Green Booster

  • Improve crop performance.
  • Increase food production.
  • Promote healthy plant development.
  • Enhance farm income.

PAGE 4: METHODS OF APPLICATION

Foliar Application

Spraying Green Booster directly onto plant leaves.

Soil Application

Applying Green Booster to the soil around plants.

Seed Treatment

Applying Green Booster before planting seeds.

PAGE 5: APPLICATION PROCEDURE

Steps

  1. Read manufacturer instructions.
  2. Measure recommended quantity.
  3. Mix with clean water if required.
  4. Apply evenly.
  5. Avoid over-application.

Precautions

  • Wear protective clothing.
  • Avoid contamination of water sources.
  • Store safely after use.
  • Keep away from children.
