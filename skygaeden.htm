🌿 Sky Garden Notes

A sky garden is a landscaped garden built on rooftops or elevated platforms that improves urban greenery and environmental quality.

📜 History of Sky Gardens

  • Inspired by the Hanging Gardens of Babylon.
  • Modern use increased with urbanization.
  • Now common in cities, hotels, and offices.

🌱 Features

  • Plants and trees
  • Walkways and seating
  • Irrigation systems
  • Drainage systems
  • Decorative elements

🌍 Benefits

Environmental

  • Improves air quality
  • Reduces heat
  • Manages rainwater

Social

  • Relaxation space
  • Improves mental health

Economic

  • Increases property value
  • Saves energy costs

⚙️ Design Considerations

  • Strong building structure
  • Waterproofing
  • Drainage system
  • Plant selection
  • Safety measures

⚠️ Challenges

  • High installation cost
  • Maintenance needs
  • Water management issues
  • Weather exposure

🛠️ Maintenance

  • Watering regularly
  • Pruning plants
  • Pest control
  • Drainage inspection

🌱 Conclusion

Sky gardens are essential for sustainable urban development, improving air quality, beauty, and environmental health.
